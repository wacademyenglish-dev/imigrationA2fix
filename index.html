STRATEGY FRAMEWORK 2

Grammar: Modals of Obligation

HAVE TO

Use have to when something is a strict rule or a necessity.

"You have to show your passport."

DON'T HAVE TO

Use don't have to when something is optional or NOT necessary.

"You don't have to print your ticket."
